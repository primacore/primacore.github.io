Who we are

Overview of Primacore Networks, LLC

Primacore Networks, LLC is a small, IT consulting firm with experience in many vertical markets, including service providers, financials, building materials, sports marketing, and education.  We also hold several, desirable certifications such as CCIE, MCSE, and PMP.

What we do

Areas of Expertise

Primacore® endeavor to fill the niche consulting required by modern enterprises to help find ways to control IT costs while still supporting business growth.  With 15 years of experience, we understand the challenges facing organizations large and small to harness the power of IT for enabling business growth, improving profitability, and eliminating the "ebb and flow" between outsourcing and in-house IT management.  We specialize in professional consulting for core Information Technology fields such as computer networks, project management, and process optimization.

When we began

A Brief Timeline

Primacore Networks originally began as an idea in 2003, after the implosion of WorldCom left other service providers, equipment manufacturers, and IT outsourcers wondering what went wrong.  As the old saying goes, "When life gives you a lemon, it's time to make lemonade."  That idea lingered through the recovery that followed over the next 6 years until late in 2009, the wheels were set in motion.  Primacore Networks, LLC was formed in March 2010 with a particular niche in mind.  After witnessing first hand over 16 years of Information Technology changes, Primacore® represents a new era of highly technical, specialized IT consultants.
