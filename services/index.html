Security Services

Securing environments large and small

The Security emphasis of Primacore® services contains vendor-specific expertise, such as firewalls, internet proxies, and authentication servers, as well as industry-wide, procedural expertise that touches all areas of the Internetwork.  For us, security is a strategy and a tactic, requiring planning from the base architecture through the stages of implementation and operations, even into the remainder of the IT lifecycle with service management and decommission.

Process Optimization

Waste not, want not

Primacore® services provide a foundational priority to the examination of processes and procedures used in the design, implementation, and operation of secure internetworks.  We follow the IT Infrastructure Library (ITIL) model for IT service management, driving best practices and auditable accountability through our services offered to our clients.  In addition, we apply the Six Sigma and Lean Six Sigma methodologies to reduce the variability of processes and improve the flow of processes.

Project Management
